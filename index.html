Biography

Moin is a drummer from India, who has transitioned into a music composer and producer. He has honed his skills and explored various music genres through his experience of playing drums alongside a wide range of artists, from Metal to hip-hop. Although he has a deep passion for creating instrumental post-rock music, he also frequently collaborates with artists to craft pop and hip-hop tracks.
