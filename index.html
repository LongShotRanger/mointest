Biography

In the bustling city of Bombay, amidst the cacophony of urban life, Moin Farooqui’s beats create a symphony that resonates far beyond the metropolis. A drummer and music producer/composer, Moin’s journey from antique stores to the stage reflects the harmonious blend of passion, perseverance, and the undeniable call of the drums.
